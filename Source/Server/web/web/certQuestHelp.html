Why are the certification questions answered this way?
 
The certification questions for your new disclosure have been answered based on your answers to the certification questions for your financial entities.
  • If you have no financial entities, both of the certifications questions will be answered as No.

  • If you answered Yes to Question 1 for any of your financial entities, Question 1 will be answered as Yes for the disclosure.

  • If you answered Yes to Question 2 for any of your financial entities, Question 2 will be answered as Yes for the disclosure.
